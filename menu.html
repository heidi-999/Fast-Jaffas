MENU

We want to make vegan food easy to find, try and enjoy- veganism suits everyone!

You don’t have to compromise anymore, just bring your friend here and everyone can find something that suits their taste.

The wow effect; “I can’t believe this is vegan!”, is the one we are looking for.

We update our menu a few times a year, choosing the ingredients by seasonal specialties. But there are always some classics

that stay on the menu, like the famous tofu limonello!
