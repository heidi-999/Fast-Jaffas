Events

The cultural offer is taken care of by the art exhibitions that change on our walls every month, as well as various music and culture-related events.

On Tuesdays we often hold a movie night upstairs.

On Thursdays there is a music quiz upstairs, unless an event has been booked in advance.

The prices include the ticket office commission.

Our events follow the safer space principles.
